Knowledge layer

Knowledge that can be trusted and reused.

KnowledgeFund helps turn scattered business information into approved, owned, reusable knowledge. This gives AI a safer source of truth than random files, chat history, or memory alone.

  • identify important knowledge
  • connect related documents and context
  • assign owners
  • mark approval status
  • retain history
Governance layer

Rules before action.

Before AI-supported work is generated, recommended, updated, or executed, KnowledgeFund applies the organisation's rules.

  • who can access what
  • what sources can be used
  • when approval is required
  • what evidence must be retained
  • what actions are draft-only versus executable

Automation layer

From knowledge to business work.

KnowledgeFund is designed to help teams complete repeatable, knowledge-heavy work with more consistency and control.

  • answer staff questions from approved sources
  • generate tender and proposal drafts
  • review documents against rules
  • create reports and summaries
  • prepare system updates
Operating flow

A simple operating flow.

1

A person asks for an answer, document, review, report, or workflow.

2

KnowledgeFund retrieves approved knowledge and relevant context.

3

Governance rules are applied before output or action.

4

AI drafts, recommends, checks, or prepares the work.

5

Humans review where required and evidence is retained.
